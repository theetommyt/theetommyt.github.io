Color

Color plays a critical role in building a brand, both in marketing and software design. A palette can be masterful at the art of persuasion and within itself carry the ability to imply, direct, and even inspire. The colors at IMS Health both capture the emotion of our brand and reflect the thoughtful experiences we create.
