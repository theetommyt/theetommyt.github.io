Modal sizes

Depending on the content being displayed in the modal, you may want to use the small or large variant. For example, a simple message prompt works well with .modal-sm, while something larger like a multi-step wizard would benefit from the added space provided by .modal-lg.

Modal with image

To use an image as a full-width cap on the modal, place the image inside .modal-image before the .modal-body. In this case, you should not use .modal-header for the title. Instead, the title goes inside the .modal-body.
