The space we operate in

The Apollo environment establishes a framework for the visual identity of our brand: consistent, effective, and distinguishable. It creates a space for improved operational efficiency with a focus on the task at hand, and subtle interaction motifs that minimize visual guess-work and distractions.

Environment

The Apollo UI lives within an enclosed, clean, light, satin-metal environment. The static appearance of the background may appear white to silvery to darkened depending on the experience.

Ambient 100%
Diffuse 75%

Metal 60%

Friction

The core material feels like it is in a 3D environment. It ﬂoats ﬂuidly along, x, y, and z-axis. Objects should have a sense of weightlessness and should ﬂoat through space with zero friction.

Gravity

Some elements have a subtle appearance of steady levitation. This is conveyed through lighting and shadows.

  • Min. space between element and background is 50px
  • There may be expressions where the z-axis is inﬁnite but in those cases, pay attention to the depth of ﬁeld speciﬁcations
  • There should never be any bounce
  • There can be very subtle hovering that is slow and steady

Lighting

Describes the inﬂuence of perceived height/spaciousness. High ceilings encourage abstract thinking and low/darkness encourage concrete thinking (focus on detail).
